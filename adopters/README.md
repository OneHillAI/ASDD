# Adopters

Projects running ASDD. Adoption is the win: every adopter is a user, a reference, and a
check on the standard. The list is also social proof that the standard is real, not aspirational.

## Add your project

When every MUST in [CONFORMANCE.md](../CONFORMANCE.md) passes:

1. Add `adopters/<your-project>.md` with your project, runtime, phase, and a link to where your conformance is checkable.
2. Open a PR adding it. Include a link to where your conformance is checkable (your workflows + config).
3. Add the badge to your README:
   ```markdown
   [![ASDD compliant v0.1](https://img.shields.io/badge/ASDD-compliant%20v0.1-2da44e)](https://github.com/OneHillAI/ASDD/blob/main/CONFORMANCE.md)
   ```

Only claim compliance once the MUSTs are met, an unverified claim is the exact failure ASDD rejects.
